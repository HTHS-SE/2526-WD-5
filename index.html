Our Company

Our company was founded in 1967 by a two-man team, co-founders Arjun Arunprasad and Andrei Mitchell. Founded in 1967, Arntheia has been one of the biggest players in the global phone case market, surpassing brands such as Apple and OtterBox. Our goal is to bring everyone in the world high-quality phone cases that are crafted with no errors. Our phone cases have been proven to be the strongest in the world, as they have been shows to provide more protection than Nokia phones, which are widely considered the strongest phones in the world.

Sourcing and Materials

Our phone cases are made from the highest quality materials that are obtained from around the globe. Some major countries that we import our materials from are China, Nigeria, and Switzerland. To be more specific, these cases are made from three types of crystals: glass, acryllic, and gemstone. Sand, along with other minerals, is heated up and shaped into molds, which creats glass crystal cases. Acryllic crystal cases are made from plastic, specifically PMMA. Gemstones are imported from other countries which have an abundance of gemstones and are used to make gemstone cases. Additionally, all three types of cases are further reinforced, which allows them to stay extremely durable.

Finances and Sponsorships

Our company is currently valued at about 25 trillion dollars, far more than companies such as NVIDIA and Alphabet Inc, and this is due to our company dominating the market share for this specific product. As shattering phones is a very common and prevalent problem due to carelessness, people turn to our company for the finest quality products. These two factors have led us to collaborate with many famous celebrities and organizations such as pop singer Ariana Grande and ASPCA (American Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals).
