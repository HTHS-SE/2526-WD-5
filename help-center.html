Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Shipping can take anywhere from 3-7 days to ship. Our materials come from very high quality manufacturers, so it takes a long time to get the quality perfect.

What is the price range of the phone cases?

The price range for these cases is anywhere between $40 and $8,000 dollars. The acryllic and glass cases are cheaper while the gemstone cases are more expensive.

Is there a warranty for these products?

Yes, there is a warranty. Customers can return their items up to a year after they are bought. We ensure the highest quality of repairs and customer satisfaction.

How durable are the phone cases?

These phone cases are extemely strong, so strong that they have been proven to outperform Nokia phones, the most unbreakable phones on the market.

Do the phone cases support all phones and brands?

Yes, our phone cases come in a variety of sizes, fitted for almost every single phone brand. Our most popular sizes are for the iPhone 14 Pro and the Google Pixel 8.
