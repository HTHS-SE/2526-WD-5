Our Story

1967

The Start

This is the date when the idea for the company first appeared. The original plan was to make simple phone cases, but it evolved into something more.


1979

Production

It was decided to create phone cases based on gems after wanting to try something unique. The idea was pitched to Shark Tank and it was successful, recieving $400,000 in exchange for 5% of the company.


1999

Exponential Growth

Production expanded and the company was featured in the Top 100 Businesses list by USA TODAY. The two founders, Arjun A. and Andrei M. officials entered the Forbes list of top billionares.


2013

Sponsorships

Sponsorships with many well known celebrities were established, such as pop star Ariana Grande, basketball athlete Damian Lillard, and famous youtuber Mark Rober.


2019

Trillionares

The founders of Arntheia reached net-worths of over a trillion dollars, making them the richest people alive. Free phones were donated to underprivileged societies along with cases from Arntheia.
